Transport

Transport within IIT campuses is designed to ensure easy mobility for students, faculty, and staff. Most IITs offer regular shuttle bus services connecting different parts of the campus, including hostels, academic buildings, and recreational facilities. Many IITs also provide bicycle rentals, allowing students to easily navigate the campus. For off-campus travel, students can rely on local auto-rickshaws or book cabs through apps, with some IITs offering discounted rides for students. Walking is a common mode of transport on the compact campuses, while some students also use their own vehicles or bikes for commuting. The transport system ensures that students can move around efficiently, balancing academic, extracurricular, and social activities.
