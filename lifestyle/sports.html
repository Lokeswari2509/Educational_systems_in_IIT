AthletIQ

Sports play a significant role in the IIT experience, with each campus offering a range of facilities and organized events to encourage physical activity and teamwork. IITs typically have well-maintained grounds and courts for popular sports like cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and tennis, along with indoor facilities for table tennis, chess, and gym activities. Inter-IIT sports tournaments are a major highlight, where students from all IITs compete in a spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition. Additionally, intra-college events and clubs help students stay active, develop leadership skills, and relieve academic stress.
