Rooms In IIT

Rooms in IIT hostels are typically designed for either single or shared occupancy, depending on the campus and year of study. They come furnished with basics like beds, study tables, chairs, and storage space. Each room is usually equipped with internet connectivity, and students often personalize their space to create a comfortable study and relaxation area.
