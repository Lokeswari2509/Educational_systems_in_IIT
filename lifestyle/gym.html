Gym

The gyms in IITs are well-equipped fitness centers designed to promote physical health and well-being among students. They provide a range of equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, resistance training machines, and spaces for group exercises like yoga or aerobics. Many IITs also have separate gyms for male and female students, and some campuses offer specialized fitness classes or personal training sessions.
