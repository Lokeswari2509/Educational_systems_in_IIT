canteen

The canteens in IITs play a central role in student life, offering a variety of food options to cater to the diverse tastes and preferences of the campus community. They are popular gathering spots where students meet to relax, socialize, and recharge between classes or study sessions. Most IIT campuses have multiple canteens spread across hostels and academic areas, providing both nutritious and budget-friendly meals. The menu typically includes a mix of North Indian, South Indian, snacks, and fast food options, with many IITs also offering international cuisines. Additionally, some IITs emphasize healthy eating, with vegetarian and vegan options available. Canteens are not only about food—they are places for students to unwind, discuss academic matters, plan events, and enjoy informal moments, making them an integral part of campus life.
