FusionFest

Fests in IITs are grand celebrations that combine culture, creativity, and innovation. Each IIT hosts its own annual cultural and technical festivals, attracting students from across the country. These fests include events like music and dance performances, technical workshops, coding competitions, quizzes, art exhibitions, and guest lectures. Popular IIT fests are known for star performances, robotics challenges, and a wide array of competitions and activities. They provide students with a chance to relax, explore new interests, and showcase their talents, making them a memorable part of IIT life.
