Welcome to IIT Dharwad

IIT Dharwad, located in Karnataka, is an emerging hub of technological innovation and academic excellence. With a focus on cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary learning, and industry collaboration, the institute equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for impactful careers. Its modern campus and dedicated faculty create an ideal learning environment.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Dharwad is an enriching journey combining academics, research, and cultural exploration. The serene campus, surrounded by greenery and hills, offers a peaceful environment conducive to focused learning and personal development.

The institute hosts flagship events like "Bhoomi," the annual cultural fest, and "TechSarigam," its technical fest. Students engage in competitions, technical workshops, and cultural performances, building teamwork and leadership skills while showcasing their talents.

Research and innovation are central to campus life, with numerous student-led projects, hackathons, and industry-sponsored research initiatives. This hands-on learning approach prepares students for professional success and entrepreneurship.

Sports and fitness are integral to student life, with facilities for cricket, football, volleyball, and indoor games like badminton and table tennis. Regular sports events and inter-college tournaments help students maintain a healthy and active lifestyle.

Nestled in the culturally rich region of Karnataka, IIT Dharwad offers students opportunities to explore historical sites like the UNESCO World Heritage site of Hampi, cultural hubs like Hubballi, and nature reserves like Dandeli. This blend of academic rigor and cultural immersion makes IIT Dharwad a unique and rewarding educational experience.
