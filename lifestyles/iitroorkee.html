Welcome to IIT Roorkee

Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, one of India's oldest and most prestigious institutions, excels in engineering, technology, and innovation. Established in 1847, it fosters a legacy of academic excellence, groundbreaking research, and global contributions. The lush green campus offers state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant learning environment, inspiring future leaders.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Roorkee combines rigorous academics with a dynamic campus experience. Renowned for its cultural and technical fests like Thomso and Cognizance, the institute offers numerous opportunities for students to showcase their talents.

The sprawling campus, with its historic architecture and serene surroundings, creates a perfect setting for growth. Students engage in diverse activities, from sports and adventure clubs to arts and music, fostering creativity and collaboration.

The hostels, with a strong sense of community, serve as the heart of student life. IIT Roorkee's vibrant environment promotes holistic development, lifelong friendships, and preparation for global challenges.
