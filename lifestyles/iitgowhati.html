Welcome to IIT Guwahati

IIT Guwahati, established in 1994, is a premier institute of technology located amidst the picturesque landscapes of Assam, near the Brahmaputra River. Renowned for its cutting-edge research, innovative academics, and serene environment, it provides a world-class education, fostering innovation and collaboration among students and faculty.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Guwahati offers a unique blend of academics, nature, and culture. The campus, surrounded by hills and a lake, provides a tranquil learning environment.

Students actively participate in events like Alcheringa, the cultural festival, and Techniche, the tech-management fest, which showcase creativity and technical prowess. The institute encourages holistic development through clubs for music, sports, literature, and coding.
