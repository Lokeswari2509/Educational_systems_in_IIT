Welcome to IIT Kanpur

Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) is one of India's premier institutions, renowned for excellence in engineering, science, and research. Established in 1959, it fosters innovation, interdisciplinary learning, and global collaborations. With a sprawling campus and cutting-edge facilities, IIT Kanpur continues to shape future leaders and technologists globally.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Kanpur is a harmonious blend of academics, extracurricular activities, and community engagement. The 1000-acre campus boasts world-class research labs, sports facilities, and serene green spaces.

Events like Techkriti (technical festival) and Antaragni (cultural fest) offer students opportunities to showcase their talents and network. The campus promotes holistic development through clubs focusing on arts, robotics, debate, and entrepreneurship.

Hostel life fosters lifelong friendships and a vibrant community spirit. With an environment encouraging innovation and collaboration, students at IIT Kanpur experience a dynamic lifestyle that balances academic rigor with personal growth and creativity.
