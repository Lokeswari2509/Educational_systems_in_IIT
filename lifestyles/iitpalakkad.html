Welcome to IIT Palakkad

IIT Palakkad, located in the lush green surroundings of Kerala, is a rapidly growing institute committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. With modern infrastructure and a research-driven approach, it provides students with a strong academic foundation and practical exposure, preparing them for global technological challenges.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Palakkad is an enriching experience, balancing rigorous academics with a vibrant campus culture. Surrounded by scenic hills and serene landscapes, the campus provides a peaceful learning environment that promotes intellectual and personal growth.

The institute hosts annual events like "Petrichor" and "Ivision," where students showcase their talents in technology, arts, and cultural activities. These fests include technical workshops, cultural performances, and social initiatives, fostering creativity and leadership skills.

A strong emphasis on research and innovation drives students toward cutting-edge projects and entrepreneurial ventures. Collaborations with industry leaders and global research institutions provide valuable exposure and career-building opportunities.

Sports and recreation are integral to campus life, with facilities for football, cricket, badminton, and table tennis. The campus encourages students to maintain a healthy lifestyle through regular sports meets, tournaments, and fitness programs.

Located in Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," IIT Palakkad offers students the chance to explore breathtaking natural attractions like the Western Ghats, Silent Valley National Park, and scenic waterfalls. This unique blend of academic excellence and natural beauty makes studying at IIT Palakkad a truly memorable experience.
