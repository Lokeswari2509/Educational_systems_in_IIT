Welcome to IIT Mandi

IIT Mandi is known for its research-driven approach to education, focusing on interdisciplinary studies in technology and engineering. Located in the picturesque Himachal Pradesh, it offers state-of-the-art infrastructure and promotes innovation, sustainability, and entrepreneurship. The institute fosters an environment that encourages critical thinking and problem-solving among students.

Campus Lifestyle

The lifestyle at IIT Mandi offers a unique blend of academic rigor and natural beauty. Nestled in the serene hills of Himachal Pradesh, students enjoy a peaceful, distraction-free environment conducive to learning.

The campus is equipped with modern hostels, labs, and recreational facilities. While academics are intense, students engage in numerous extracurricular activities like sports, cultural fests, and technical workshops.

The institute also encourages innovation, with students actively involved in research projects and startups. The surrounding mountains and scenic landscapes provide opportunities for adventure and relaxation, making life at IIT Mandi not just academically rewarding, but also deeply connected to nature.
