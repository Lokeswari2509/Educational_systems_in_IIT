Welcome to IIT Jodhpur

IIT Jodhpur, located in the vibrant city of Jodhpur, Rajasthan, is a hub of technological excellence and innovation. With cutting-edge research facilities and a focus on interdisciplinary learning, it fosters an environment where creativity and knowledge flourish. Its curriculum integrates technology, humanities, and management, promoting holistic development.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Jodhpur is an enriching experience that blends academic pursuits with cultural and social activities. The state-of-the-art campus offers modern hostels, advanced laboratories, and extensive sports and recreation facilities. Students participate in cultural fests, tech events, and entrepreneurial ventures, creating a dynamic campus life.

Surrounded by the historical and cultural richness of Rajasthan, students can explore iconic landmarks like Mehrangarh Fort and Umaid Bhawan Palace. The serene desert landscape adds a unique charm to campus life, making the learning experience at IIT Jodhpur both academically fulfilling and culturally immersive.

The institute emphasizes all-around development, promoting leadership and teamwork through clubs and student organizations. Various interest-based societies focus on technology, arts, literature, and entrepreneurship, helping students develop skills beyond academics.
