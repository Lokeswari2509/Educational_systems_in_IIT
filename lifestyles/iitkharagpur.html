Welcome to IIT Kharagpur

IIT Kharagpur, the first Indian Institute of Technology, established in 1951, is a hub of academic excellence and innovation. Located in Kharagpur, West Bengal, it offers cutting-edge education, groundbreaking research opportunities, and a sprawling 2,100-acre campus that nurtures creativity and holistic development in students from diverse backgrounds.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Kharagpur is a dynamic blend of academics, culture, and recreation. The lush green campus, complete with modern amenities, fosters a vibrant student community. Celebrated events like Spring Fest and Kshitij bring cultural and technological brilliance to the forefront, attracting participants from across the globe.

The campus offers excellent sports facilities, including a gymkhana, swimming pools, and courts for various sports.

Active student clubs in robotics, music, photography, and debate provide creative outlets. Hostel life is full of camaraderie, with inter-hall competitions adding to the excitement. IIT Kharagpur ensures holistic development alongside academic excellence.
