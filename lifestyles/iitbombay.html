Welcome to IIT Bombay

IIT Bombay is renowned for its academic rigor and vibrant student life. Nestled in the picturesque Powai area of Mumbai, it offers an ecosystem of excellence, collaboration, and innovation.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at IIT Bombay is a blend of academics, culture, and personal growth. The institute is home to one of the most active student bodies in India, hosting events like the annual cultural fest *Mood Indigo* and the internationally acclaimed *Techfest*. These events bring together students, professionals, and artists from across the globe.

The lush green campus, surrounded by the serene Powai Lake, provides a tranquil environment for learning. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy world-class facilities, including a swimming pool, gym, cricket ground, and tennis courts. The institute promotes holistic development through its active clubs for music, dance, debate, and more.

IIT Bombay's hostels are hubs of energy, fostering camaraderie and lifelong friendships. Each hostel has its own identity and hosts inter-hostel competitions, making campus life vibrant and competitive.
