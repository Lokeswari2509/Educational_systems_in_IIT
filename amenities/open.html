Open Air Theatre

The Open Air Theatre (OAT) in IITs is a popular venue for cultural events, performances, and gatherings, offering a unique outdoor experience. It is often used for movie screenings, live music performances, plays, and various other student activities. The OAT provides an excellent atmosphere for students to come together, relax, and enjoy events under the stars. With its casual and open setting, it serves as a space where students can showcase their talents or unwind after a long day of studies and work. The OAT is an integral part of the social and cultural life at IITs, creating memorable experiences for all who attend.
