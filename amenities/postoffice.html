PostOffice

The post office in IIT campuses serves as a convenient facility for students and staff to send and receive mail, packages, and important documents. It handles various postal services like registered posts, speed posts, and parcel deliveries, ensuring smooth communication both within and outside the campus. The IIT post office often provides additional services like postal banking, bill payments, and stamp sales, making it an essential service for the campus community. It also acts as a point of contact for students living in hostels, offering a reliable way to manage personal correspondence.
