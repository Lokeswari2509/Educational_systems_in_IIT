Amenities of IIT Kharagpur

IIT Kharagpur, the first of the IITs, offers a blend of heritage and modern facilities. The campus houses advanced laboratories, one of the largest libraries in India, and cutting-edge lecture halls to create an enriching academic environment for its students.

Set in a vast, green campus, IIT Kharagpur provides excellent sports and recreational amenities. The institute features multiple sports fields, a swimming pool, and a gymkhana that encourages students to actively participate in extracurricular and sporting activities.

Additionally, the campus offers world-class amenities, including spacious hostels, diverse dining options, and robust healthcare services. The vibrant campus life and holistic infrastructure make IIT Kharagpur a leading institution for academic and personal development.
