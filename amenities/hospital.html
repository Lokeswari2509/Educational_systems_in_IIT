Hospital

IITs have on-campus medical facilities to cater to the health and wellness needs of students, staff, and faculty. The institutes are equipped with basic healthcare services, including outpatient care, first aid, and emergency medical assistance. Many IITs also have tie-ups with nearby hospitals for specialized treatments and referrals. The medical centers offer routine checkups, vaccinations, and preventive care, while also providing support for mental health and counseling services. In case of severe emergencies, students are referred to nearby hospitals for more advanced care. The aim is to ensure that students have access to proper healthcare and are supported throughout their academic journey.
