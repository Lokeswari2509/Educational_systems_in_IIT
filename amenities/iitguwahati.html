Amenities of IIT Guwahati

IIT Guwahati, situated amidst the serene backdrop of the Brahmaputra River and the hills, offers top-notch academic and research facilities. With state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, and modern lecture halls, the institute fosters an environment of innovation and intellectual growth.

The campus is equipped with world-class sports facilities, including athletic tracks, a swimming pool, and dedicated courts for tennis and basketball. IIT Guwahati encourages students to engage in sports and extracurricular activities, ensuring a healthy balance between academics and recreation.

The institute also provides comprehensive amenities such as modern hostels, diverse dining options, and accessible healthcare services. With its breathtaking natural setting and vibrant campus life, IIT Guwahati offers a unique and enriching experience for its students and staff.
