Amenities of IIT Bhubaneswar

IIT Bhubaneswar offers world-class facilities designed to foster academic excellence and innovation. The campus is equipped with modern lecture halls, advanced research labs, and a well-stocked library. Students benefit from cutting-edge resources that support their academic endeavors and research projects.

The sprawling campus also features a wide range of recreational facilities, including sports complexes, gymnasiums, and playgrounds. IIT Bhubaneswar encourages students to participate in various sports and fitness activities, helping them maintain a healthy and active lifestyle alongside their academic pursuits.

The institute provides essential amenities such as dining facilities, shopping areas, and healthcare services, ensuring that students' needs are met on campus. The combination of excellent infrastructure and vibrant campus life makes IIT Bhubaneswar an ideal place for both academic growth and personal development.
