Amenities of IIT Roorkee

IIT Roorkee, one of India's premier institutions, is equipped with exceptional facilities to nurture academic and personal growth. The campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, well-stocked libraries, and advanced lecture halls to provide a conducive environment for learning and innovation.

Set amidst picturesque surroundings, IIT Roorkee offers top-notch sports and recreational facilities, including a swimming pool, tennis courts, and a modern gymnasium. These facilities encourage students to stay active and maintain a balanced lifestyle while pursuing their studies.

The institute also provides essential amenities such as well-maintained hostels, diverse dining options, and comprehensive medical services. With a vibrant student community and world-class infrastructure, IIT Roorkee offers an enriching experience for all its residents.
