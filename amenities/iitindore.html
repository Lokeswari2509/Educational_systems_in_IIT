Amenities of IIT Indore

IIT Indore, one of the newer IITs, is known for its modern infrastructure and focus on research-driven education. The campus features advanced laboratories, a rich digital library, and technology-enabled lecture halls that promote a world-class academic experience.

The institute provides excellent facilities for sports and recreation, including a multi-sport complex, a gymnasium, and well-maintained courts for basketball, volleyball, and more. Students are encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities for a holistic campus experience.

IIT Indore also offers comfortable residential facilities with modern hostels, dining options catering to diverse preferences, and comprehensive healthcare services. With its state-of-the-art amenities and serene surroundings, the institute provides a nurturing environment for personal and professional growth.
