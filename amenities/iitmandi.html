Amenities of IIT Mandi

IIT Mandi is equipped with modern academic facilities, including well-designed classrooms, advanced laboratories, and a well-stocked library. These facilities help students engage in high-quality learning and research in a conducive environment.

The campus offers excellent recreational and sports facilities, such as sports complexes, gymnasiums, and outdoor spaces. IIT Mandi encourages students to take part in various sports and physical activities, helping them lead a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

IIT Mandi also provides essential amenities, including dining options, shopping centers, and healthcare facilities, ensuring students have a comfortable living experience. The campus is designed to foster academic, personal, and social growth for all students.
