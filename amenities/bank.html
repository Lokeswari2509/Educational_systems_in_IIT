bank

IITs have campus-based banking facilities to cater to the financial needs of students, faculty, and staff. These banks provide a wide range of services such as savings accounts, ATM facilities, loans, and financial advice. The most common bank branches on IIT campuses include nationalized banks like **State Bank of India (SBI)**, **Punjab National Bank (PNB)**, and **Bank of India (BOI)**. These banks typically have ATM machines across the campus for easy access to cash. Additionally, most IITs have online banking services, enabling students to manage their finances, pay fees, and transfer money conveniently. Some IITs also have tie-ups with digital payment platforms for seamless transactions. These banking services help students with day-to-day financial transactions and manage their allowances, scholarships, and any other financial requirements.
