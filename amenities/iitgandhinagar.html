Amenities of IIT Gandhinagar

IIT Gandhinagar provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic learning and research. The campus features modern classrooms, cutting-edge laboratories, and a well-stocked library with digital resources. These facilities enable students to engage in innovative learning and cutting-edge research projects.

The campus offers an array of sports and recreational amenities, including sports complexes, gymnasiums, and open spaces. IIT Gandhinagar promotes physical activity among students, ensuring they maintain a healthy and active lifestyle alongside their academic pursuits.

In addition, IIT Gandhinagar provides essential amenities such as dining options, shopping areas, and healthcare services to ensure students' well-being. The vibrant campus life and excellent infrastructure make IIT Gandhinagar an ideal place for both academic and personal growth.
