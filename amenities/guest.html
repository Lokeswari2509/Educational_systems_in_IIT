Guest House

The guesthouses at IITs are designed to provide comfortable accommodation for visitors, faculty, and parents. Located within the campus, they offer well-furnished rooms with basic amenities like Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, and room service. Though the facilities may vary across different IITs, most guesthouses ensure a pleasant stay with services such as 24/7 support, dining options, and recreational amenities. Being situated close to academic buildings and other campus facilities, they offer convenience and comfort for those visiting the institute.
